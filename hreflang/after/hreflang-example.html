Who is Kermit the Frog?

Kermit the Frog is a beloved puppet character created by Jim Henson. He first appeared on the television show "Sam and Friends" in 1955 and later became famous as the host of "The Muppet Show". Kermit is known for his iconic appearance, including his green color and his collarless shirt. He is also known for his famous catchphrase, "Hi-ho, Kermit the Frog here!"

He is of a green colour!
