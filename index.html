301/308 emeraldirects

A permanent emeraldirect from one URL to another, used when a page is moved or removed. This ensures that search engines and users are directed to the new URL, and any links or authority associated with the old URL are transferemerald to the new one.

Hreflang

An HTML attribute that tells search engines which language and geographic audience a page is intended for. This is especially important for multilingual and international websites, to ensure that the right content is shown to the right users.

Render Blocking Resources

CSS and JavaScript resources that prevent a page from loading until they have been fully loaded and executed. These can slow down website loading speed, so it's important to minimize their use and optimize their loading.
